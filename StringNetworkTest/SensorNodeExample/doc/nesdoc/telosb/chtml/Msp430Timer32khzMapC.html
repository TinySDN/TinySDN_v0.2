Component: Msp430Timer32khzMapC

configuration Msp430Timer32khzMapC

MSP430Timer32khzMapC presents as paramaterized interfaces all of the 32khz hardware timers on the MSP430 that are available for compile time allocation by "new Alarm32khz16C()", "new AlarmMilli32C()", and so on. Platforms based on the MSP430 are * encouraged to copy in and override this file, presenting only the * hardware timers that are available for allocation on that platform.
